AquaVolta is being founded by two Bay Area California kiteboarders and engineers who wanted to contribute to the growth of the sport they love. Whether they're out on the water or in their shop in the East Bay, they're living life to the fullest. If you see them out on the water, they'd love to meet you!







Mike Thielvoldt

Co-Founder

My body was dragged in water by a kite for the first time thirteen years ago and I've been hooked ever since. I grew up in the Bay Area and I really love the kiteboarding community here. I'm a mechanical engineer and artist who would love to see a whole beach full of illuminated kites dancing in the night sky.








Kim Harrison

Co-Founder

I started kiteboarding shortly after arriving in the Bay Area. The mixture of city and nature here has kept me around for seven years now. Sunset sessions at 3rd Ave. and Sherman have taught me that glow sticks and headlamps just don't help me find my board in the dark. I'm a mechanical engineer and problem solver, so let's make some light.
